# Food Delivery app database

Requirements:

1. Place Orders :: Customers can choose a restaurant and place an order for food from that restaurant 
2. Menu Items : Each rest has different menu items and prices, out of Scope >> customising the order by adding or removing the ingredients
3. Delivery Time : Customers can specify a delivery time, either ASAP, or at a future time
4. Delivery Address : Customers can store multiple delivery addresses in theior profile
5. Order Status : Restaurrants can accept the order, and update the status of the order as it is made
6. Assign order : Delivery drivers can pick an order to deliver to assign it to them selves
7. pick up order : Delivery drivers can pick up an order that is ready from the restaurant and deliver it to the customer 
8. Rationgs : customers can rate driversd and restaurants
